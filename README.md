***
#### ***Description***
Oomerz is a trivia-based app where users can register and login to their profiles. After logging in users are taken to their profile screen , where they can caption their trivia attempts and then answer a series of questions in different generations of pop culture. After their trivia is completed , they are taken back to the home screen , and will receive a score category based in the majority of correct answers in a certain generation of culture. They can also view other users' profiles , their own profile , as well as change their profile pictures. Welcome to Oomerz!
